Frequently Asked
Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions I receive as a freelance designer. If you don't see your question here, feel free to reach out—I'm happy to help!

  • 1. What services do you offer?

    I specialize in UI/UX design, branding, web design, and development. Whether you need a complete website, a visual identity, or design consultation, I'm here to help bring your ideas to life.

  • 2. How does the design process work?

    My process begins with discovery and planning, where I understand your goals and audience. Then, I move on to concept creation, revisions based on your feedback, and finalize with testing and delivery. It's a collaborative process, and I keep you updated every step of the way.

  • 3. How long does a project usually take?

    The timeline varies depending on the project's scope and complexity. Small projects can take a few weeks, while larger builds might take a few months. A clear timeline will be provided during the discovery phase.

  • 4. What do I need to provide before starting a project?

    It's helpful to have a clear idea of your goals, target audience, and any existing brand assets like logos or copy. Don't worry if you don't have everything yet; we can figure it out together!

  • 5. Do you offer revisions?

    Absolutely! I value your feedback and structurally include revision rounds at key milestones to ensure the final product perfectly hits the mark.

  • 6. How do I get started?

    Just reach out through the contact form below or send me an email. We'll schedule a brief intro call to chat about your needs and see if we're a good fit!
